About us

NVideo is Europe's largest electronics trade group, Founded in 1990, it operates in 35 countries and sells quality consumer electronics and electronics in more than 11,000 shops. Today NVideo offers household appliances, electronics, telecom and computer products to more than 600 million consumers across Europe. The NVideo brand was launched in Estonia in 2003 and currently has 16 shops.
